About Richard “Grant” Nelson

  • Richard “Grant” Nelson is a Managing Director, Head of Research and Due Diligence at North Capital.
  • Private Securities. Prior to joining North Capital, Mr. Nelson served in the United States Air Force as an avionics technician, earning the rank of Senior Airman.
  • His experience also includes an internship as an analyst for the Utah Shakespeare Festival, during which time he specialized in statistics.
